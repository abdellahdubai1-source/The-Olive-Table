Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Refined for Dubai

The Olive Table began with a single branch, passed down through generations, and a belief that the finest meals start long before they reach the table. We press our own oils, proof our breads overnight, and grind our spices by hand — honoring the slow rituals of the Mediterranean coast.

Today, that heritage meets the precision and warmth of modern Dubai. Every plate is a quiet conversation between old-world patience and new-world craft, served beneath the same golden light that falls over an olive grove at dusk.
